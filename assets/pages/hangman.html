Welcome to Hang Snowman, where fun and challenge come together! Hang Snowman is our latest creation, developed collaboratively with talented group mates.

Hang Snowman was crafted through a meticulous process starting with wire framing our ideas, followed by designing detailed high-fidelity mockups using Figma. We meticulously planned every aspect, from layout to user interaction, ensuring a seamless gaming experience.
Once our designs were finalized, we delved into development with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Through countless iterations and collaborative efforts, we implemented intuitive controls, captivating graphics. To enhance Hang Snowman's functionality, we integrated a PostgreSQL database to store game data, hints, and player scores. Leveraging the power of SQL, we efficiently managed game state, ensuring a smooth and immersive gameplay experience for every user.

Join us on this exhilarating journey into the world of word games, where creativity meets technical expertise. Hang Snowman is a testament to our passion for crafting innovative digital experiences, and we're excited to share it with you. Let's build memories, one word at a time!
