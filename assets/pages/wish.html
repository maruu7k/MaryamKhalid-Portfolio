Welcome to Wish, a heartfelt project dedicated to spreading joy through toy donations! From conceptualization to execution, Wish was a solo endeavor where I wore multiple hats, handling every aspect of the project with care and dedication.

The journey of Wish began with meticulous wire framing, where I outlined the website's structure and functionality. Moving forward, I transitioned to designing detailed high-fidelity mockups, crafting a cohesive visual identity and user experience with my own style guide.
As a passionate developer, I brought Wish to life using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, infusing it with interactivity and charm. Every line of code was a labor of love, aimed at creating a seamless platform for toy donations and spreading smiles.

Wish isn't just a website; it's a testament to my dedication to making a difference in the world. With a slogan like "Tend to Wish," the project embodies the spirit of kindness and generosity, inviting users to join me in making wishes come true for children in need. This heartfelt journey with Wish, where every click has the power to brighten a child's day. Let's make wishes come true, one toy at a time!
