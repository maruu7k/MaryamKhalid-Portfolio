Welcome to our VR project, an immersive journey through the Seven Wonders of the World! This project was a collaborative effort, combining our passion for virtual reality with our fascination with historical landmarks.

Our journey began with meticulous planning and design using Figma. We crafted detailed wireframes, developed a comprehensive style guide, and brought our vision to life with high-fidelity mockups. Leveraging Figma's powerful features, we designed every aspect of the VR experience, from user interface elements to interactive components.
Transitioning from design to development, we turned to Unity, a leading platform for creating immersive experiences. Integrating Figma components into Unity, we seamlessly translated our designs into a fully functional VR environment. Utilizing Unity's capabilities, we incorporated 3D assets to recreate the Seven Wonders in stunning detail, allowing users to explore these iconic landmarks from the comfort of their own home. We included a library section where users can access detailed information and historical narratives for each Wonder, providing context and enriching the overall experience.
